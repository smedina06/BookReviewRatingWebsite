Book Review & Rating Website

under construction

My project will be a website where I review books and give them ratings based on my opinion. Each review will include the book title, author, genre, a short summary, favorite quotes, and my rating. The goal of the website is to help readers find new books and learn more about whether a book might interest them. The website will include sections such as a homepage, reviews page, favorite books page, and genre categories.

  • Home Page: Introduces the website and explains its purpose. It will welcome visitors and highlight featured book reviews.
  • Book Reviews Page: Contains detailed reviews of different books including summaries, favorite quotes, and my personal opinions.
  • Top Rated Books Page: Displays the highest rated books on the website along with star ratings and short descriptions.
  • Genres Page: Organizes book recommendations by genre such as mystery, fantasy, romance, and nonfiction.
  • About Page: Provides information about me and explains why I enjoy reading and reviewing books.
  • Contact Page: Allows visitors to send feedback or book suggestions through a contact form or email link.
